# Graveyard Shift 
*Survive the night.*

## The Night Shift
You are a caretaker of time and space, stationed in the deep cosmos. You've just clocked in for your night shift, only to discover that the **BANANA CORP** mainframes have been infected by a malicious virus. Your terminal is now flooded with dangerous pop-up windows, and it is your job to resolve them before they destroy entire galaxies.

## Employee Duties
- **Captcha Defense:** You must quickly resolve an endless stream of captcha pop-up windows to maintain the safety of the galaxies.
- **Time Travel:** There is a special window that allows you to turn back time. However, use it with extreme caution. Frequent use is highly dangerous, and previous employees have reported severe dizziness.
- **Virtual Pet Protocol:** You have a virtual pet monkey. Feeding it is mandatory. If you neglect it and it starves, you die.
